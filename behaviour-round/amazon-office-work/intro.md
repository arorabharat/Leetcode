Hi, I work on the **Amazon India Prime Team**, where I manage the Prime subscription lifecycle for customers across the India marketplace. On a day-to-day basis, I oversee various stages of the customer journey. This includes the initial sign-up process and enabling customers to select the correct payment instruments for their Prime purchase.

A key part of my role involves ensuring that **recurring charges and auto-renewals** are set up correctly so that memberships continue seamlessly. If issues arise near a plan's expiry, I work to ensure we display the appropriate UI so customers can resolve payment errors before their benefits lapse.

To provide a best-in-class acquisition and retention experience, I collaborate daily with the **Subscription, Ordering, and Payments teams** (including Cards and UPI Autopay). Additionally, I ensure that Prime services remain compliant with all regulatory requirements from the **RBI and NPCI**, such as mandating that Prime purchases are processed via **tokenized cards**.
