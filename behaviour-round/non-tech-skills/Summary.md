### Recommended Preparation Approach

Use **STAR format** for most answers:

* Situation
* Task
* Action
* Result (quantify impact: latency ↓ 30%, cost ↓ 20%, conversion ↑ 5%)

If useful, next step can be:

* map **your past projects** to these categories
* create **ready-to-use story bank (8–10 stories)** covering most questions.
